VEX Robotics Education Platform

Learn, build, and compete with Vexify

Structured curriculum, class management, real-time messaging, and a reward system — built for VEX teams.

Features
📖
Structured Lessons
30 lessons across Foundations, Design & CAD, and Programming. Read, learn, then mark complete to earn VCoins.
📅
Class Calendar
Teachers schedule lessons by date. Students see what's coming and track what they've completed.
💬
Real-time Messaging
Each class has a live chat channel powered by Firebase. Everyone stays in sync.
🎫
Help Tickets
Students submit support tickets. Teachers track, prioritize, and resolve them in one place.
🪙
VCoins & Shop
Earn coins by completing lessons and class work. Spend them in the shop on badges and rewards.
📈
Progress Tracking
Teachers see every student's lesson completion rate and VCoins earned at a glance.
